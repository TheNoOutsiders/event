EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW

FAQ

What's the dress code? +
Anything you want — just look fly. The vibe is exclusive and elevated, so dress for the moment. Whether that's smart casual, glam, or your boldest fit, come ready to be photographed.
Is there an age limit? +
18+ only. Valid government-issued photo ID required at the door. No exceptions. Please don't bring underage guests — they will be turned away and no refunds will be issued.
What's the refund policy? +
No refunds — all sales final. Tickets are non-transferable. We curate the room carefully, so please only book if you're committed to attending.
Is re-entry allowed? +
Yes — re-entry is allowed with your wristband. Keep it secure throughout the night. Wristband loss means re-entry will be refused.
How do I get there? Is there parking? +
Parking is limited at the venue. We strongly recommend using Uber, Pathao, or a cab for the smoothest arrival. Check the venue section above for the Google Maps link.
What happens after I book? +
You'll receive a unique QR code immediately after registration. Screenshot it or save it — that's your entry pass. At the door, present your QR code along with valid ID matching your booking name.
